This document proposes the design and publishing of a web application which serves as a one-stop site for anyone interested in purchasing a flat as an investment, or selling their current flat. The project aims to accomplish the following goals: Display the findings of analysis on the elements that contribute to a higher (or lower) resale price of a HDB flat. Provide the service to predict the pricing of a flat, given relevant information of the flat, and the existing infrastructure in the estate. For the first goal, the resale prices of flats, as well as basic flat information will be retrieved from data.gov. After which, more information like the location of malls, schools, healthcare and hawker centers will be added to our dataset. Following the acquisition of these datasets, we then separate flats by the type (i.e. 3-Room, 4-Room). These types will be analyzed individually, comparing the resale prices of respective flat types to every other element in our dataset in an attempt to find correlation. Any confusion between causality of elements will be discussed with our client sponsor and representatives of the Housing Development Board and rectification of any false positive will be done. For the second goal, a prediction model will be trained to predict the likely valuation of any HDB flat given basic information about the property - location, levels, housing type, housing generation and age. Subsequently, the dataset will be split into mature and developing estates. It is very likely that a currently developing estate will have a similar growth as mature estates that were once developing as well. As such, while mature estates will be analyzed for facilities and amenities nearby, it should be expected of developing estates to have slightly fewer amenities. Further analysis will have to be performed to determine the trends, as well as factors affecting these estates in order to create a prediction model for the resale value of flats in developing and mature estates. The main constraints for our project would be the accuracy of data, as well as the noise within the dataset. Dataset with more noise will require more time to be cleaned and analyzed. Also, we will be focusing on more tangible things to analyze like the facilities and amenities (i.e. Religious buildings, schools, hawker centers, shopping malls), and will not be analyzing any other things. Excluded criteria will include scenery, the sun direction or condition of each house prior to selling.
